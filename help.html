Getting Started

The Enhanced Weather App provides real-time weather data, forecasts, and air quality information to help you plan your day. Below is a guide on how to use the app and its features.

How to Use the App

1. Search for a City

Use the Search button to find weather details for any city:

  • Enter the name of the city in the search bar (e.g., "New York" or "London").
  • Click the Search button to fetch the weather data.
  • Details displayed include:
    • Temperature
    • Weather description (e.g., sunny, cloudy)
    • Sunrise and sunset times
    • Wind speed
    • Humidity percentage
    • Air Quality Index (AQI)

2. Use My Location

Click the Use My Location button to fetch weather details for your current location:

  • Ensure location services are enabled on your device and allow the app to access your location.
  • The app automatically detects your location and displays the weather details.

Understanding the Menu

The app's menu provides quick navigation to key sections:

  • Home: Returns you to the main page where you can search for weather data or use location-based services.
  • About: Learn more about the app, its features, and the team behind it.
  • Help: Access this page for guidance on how to use the app effectively.
  • Toggle Dark Mode: Switch between Light and Dark themes for a comfortable viewing experience.

Features Summary

The app is packed with features to provide a complete weather experience:

  • Current Weather: Get live updates for any location worldwide.
  • 24-Hour Forecast: Plan your day with hourly weather data.
  • Air Quality Index: Stay informed about air pollution levels.
  • Historical Weather Data: Analyze past weather trends for your city.
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.
  • Light & Dark Mode: Adjust the app's appearance to suit your preference.

Troubleshooting

  • Search Not Working: Ensure you've entered a valid city name. Check your internet connection.
  • Location Not Detected: Enable location services and grant the app permission to access your location.
  • Dark Mode Issues: If the toggle button isn't working, refresh the page or clear your browser cache.
