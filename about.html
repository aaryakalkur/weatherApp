About This App

The Enhanced Weather App is designed to provide users with real-time weather updates, accurate forecasts, and detailed air quality information. Our mission is to make weather information accessible and easy to understand for everyone.

Features

  • **Current Weather**: Get live weather updates for any city around the world.
  • **24-Hour Forecast**: Plan ahead with hourly weather predictions.
  • **Historical Weather Data**: Analyze past weather trends.
  • **Air Quality Index (AQI)**: Stay informed about air pollution levels.
  • **Light & Dark Mode**: Customize the app's appearance to suit your preference.
  • **Responsive Design**: Optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.

How It Works

The app fetches data from trusted weather APIs like OpenWeatherMap. By entering a city name, users can access real-time updates and other details. Our application processes this data and displays it in an intuitive and user-friendly interface.

Our Vision

We aim to empower users with reliable weather data to help them make informed decisions, whether it’s planning daily activities, ensuring safety during severe weather, or staying updated on environmental conditions.

Technology Stack

This app is built using modern web development technologies:

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: For structure, styling, and interactivity.
  • Weather API Integration: Provides real-time and forecast data.
  • Responsive Design: Ensures seamless functionality on any device.
  • Local Storage: Saves user preferences like Dark Mode.
